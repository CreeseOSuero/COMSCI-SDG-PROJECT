GOAL 16 - PEACE, JUSTICE, AND STRONG INSTITUTIONS

What is SDG's Goal 16?

Goal 16 is about advocating peaceful and inclusive societies while providing access to justice for all. With this it also advocates for building effective, accountable, and inclusive institutions everywhere. People should also be free of all fear from any form of violence- feeling safe no matter what their faith, ethnicity, or sexual orientation is.
