SDG 16 CAMPAIGN : PEACE IN OUR CITIES

What is the campaign about?

The SDG 16 campaign, "Peace in Our Cities Campaign" is a global initiative which aims to reduce urban violence, create more evidence informed policies, and accompany various people in order for them to realize ambitious targets for violence reduction. It mostly focuses on promoting peace and security globally.
