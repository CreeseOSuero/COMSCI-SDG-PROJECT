GOAL 16 - PEACE, JUSTICE, AND STRONG INSTITUTIONS

This website aims to uphold goal 16 of SDG which is to promote just, peaceful, and inclusive societies. Lately, there have been many cases of injustice causing many problems. These problems do not just extend to just an individual but also our country, the Philippines

Therefore, this website (again) aims to uphold goal 16 of SDG. With webpages that contain information regarding goal 16 and what we could do to reach this goal. Though it may seem impossible, with small and simple actions, we can make goal 16 possible! We can have peace, justice, and strong institutions.

Target 16.1 : Reduce violence everywhere

Target 16.2 : Protect chidren from abuse, exploitation, trafficking, and violence

Target 16.3 : Equal access to justice

Target 16.4 : Combat organized crime and illicit financial and arms flows

Target 16.5 : Substantially reduce corruption and bribery

Target 16.6 : Develop effective, accountable, and transparent institutions

Target 16.7 : Ensure responsive, inclusive, and representative decision-making

Target 16.8 : Strengthen the participation in global governance

Target 16.9 : Provide universal legal identity

Target 16.10 : Ensure public access to information and protect fundamental freedoms

Target 16.a : Strengthen national institutions to prevent violence and combat crime and terrorism

Target 16.10 : Promote and enfore non-discriminatory laws and policies
